HOMES CURFEW PASS

The GCH Curfew Pass Request System is a web-based platform developed to assist students residing in GCH with a secure and convenient way to request permission for late-night entry into the subdivision.

This system aims to balance the security protocols of GCH with the real-life needs of students who may need to return past curfew due to internships, academic projects, emergencies, or other valid reasons.
